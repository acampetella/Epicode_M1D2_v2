Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the plan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife id desired, though it's not necessary))
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. Ypou don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate ther last of the flour...
